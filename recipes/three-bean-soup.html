Three Bean Soup

Description

This easy three bean soup is hearty and packed full of protein, just perfect for a cosy weeknight dinner. All you need is three types of canned beans, and you'll be ready to face any chilly evening.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 1 medium carrot, diced
  • 2 celery stalkes, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced or pressed
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 1 x 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 x 400g canned cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 x 400g canned black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 x 400g canned pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 liter vegetable stock
  • Handful baby spinach
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped parsley
  • Juice of whole lemon

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot and saute the onion, carrot and celery for 6-7 minutes, stirring continuously, until slightly softened.
  2. Next, add the garlic and red chilli flakes and cook for another minute until fragrant. Stir in the chopped tomatoes and oregano and continue to cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Stir in all three types of beans and vegetable stock, bring to a boil, then lower the heat and simmer for 20 minutes until the beans are tender and the soup has naturally thickened a bit.
  4. Stir in the baby spinach, parsley and lemon juice and cover with a lid for 2-3 minutes to allow the spinach to wilt.
  5. Serve with extra lemon wedges and crusty bread.
