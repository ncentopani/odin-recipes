Fettucine Aglio e Olio

Description

Pasta aglio e olio is a great recipe when you're in need of a quick, simple and delicious meal! After trying this recipe you won't believe it only uses 5 simple ingredients! I prefer to use fettucine, but you can substitute any long pasta of your liking!

ingredients

  • 1 pound uncooked Fettucine
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsely
  • 1 cup finely grated Parmigiano cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
